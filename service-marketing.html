Service Overview

Successful salons do not rely on random promotions or inconsistent social media activity. They grow through clear strategy, structured execution, and performance tracking.

Our Salon Marketing System provides a complete, systemized approach to demand generation, client acquisition, and retention — aligned with your salon's positioning, pricing, and growth goals.

This service transforms marketing from guesswork into a measurable growth engine.

  • Premium Positioning

  • Scaling Operations

What Is Included in the Salon Marketing System?

Our marketing framework is built on five essential growth pillars that support sustainable and profitable salon growth.

1 Brand Positioning & Marketing Strategy

Clear positioning that attracts the right clients — not just more clients.

  • Brand and market positioning analysis
  • Target client definition and behavior mapping
  • Competitor and pricing alignment
  • Service focus and hero treatments strategy
  • Marketing tone and communication guidelines

2 Marketing SOPs & Execution Systems

Structured procedures that define how marketing is planned, executed, and controlled.

  • Marketing planning and campaign SOP
  • Content creation and posting SOP
  • Promotion and offer approval SOP
  • Brand consistency and quality control SOP
  • Crisis and negative feedback handling SOP

3 Lead Generation & Booking Conversion System

Marketing must convert into bookings — not just engagement.

  • Lead handling and response time standards
  • Booking conversion SOP (online, phone, WhatsApp, DM)
  • Reception and sales alignment SOP
  • First-visit and consultation conversion framework
  • Follow-up and no-show reduction process

4 Client Retention & Loyalty Marketing

Growth is protected through repeat visits and long-term relationships.

  • Client follow-up and aftercare SOP
  • Rebooking and retention campaigns
  • Loyalty and referral program structure
  • Database segmentation and CRM usage
  • VIP and high-value client strategy

5 Marketing Performance & ROI Control

What gets measured gets improved.

  • Marketing KPI definitions and benchmarks
  • Campaign performance tracking reports
  • Cost-per-lead and booking analysis
  • Channel effectiveness evaluation
  • Monthly marketing review and optimization framework

Role-Specific Marketing Responsibilities

  • Management & Owner Marketing SOP

    Marketing planning and budget control, campaign approval and brand compliance, performance analysis and reporting, agency or freelancer coordination, strategic growth decision-making

  • Reception & Front Desk Marketing SOP

    Handling marketing leads and inquiries, converting promotions into bookings, capturing client data accurately, upselling campaigns and rebooking offers, feedback collection and follow-up
