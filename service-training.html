Service Overview

A salon's success is driven by people, performance, and consistency.

Our Staff Training System for Sales & Customer Service is designed to ensure every team member — from reception to management — delivers a premium client experience, drives revenue ethically, and follows clear performance standards.

This training system transforms talent into a structured, accountable, and high-performing team.

What Is Included in the Staff Training System?

Our training framework is built on four essential pillars that ensure service excellence, sales performance, and operational discipline.

1 Customer Service Excellence SOP

Clear standards that define how clients are welcomed, consulted, served, and retained.

  • Professional greeting and first impression standards
  • Client communication and consultation techniques
  • Managing expectations and explaining services clearly
  • Handling objections, complaints, and difficult situations
  • Client care during and after the service
  • Rebooking and follow-up etiquette

Ensures a consistent, premium client experience at every touchpoint.

2 Sales & Revenue SOP (Ethical, Client-Centered)

Structured sales training focused on trust, value, and long-term retention — not pressure selling.

  • Understanding client needs and recommending solutions
  • Service upgrades and add-ons (when appropriate)
  • Retail product recommendations and education
  • Rebooking strategies and loyalty building
  • Sales language aligned with premium positioning
  • Performance tracking and conversion awareness

Supports higher revenue, better retention, and brand integrity.

3 Role-Specific Training SOPs

Reception & Front Desk Training SOP

Reception is the sales and experience hub of the salon.

  • Call handling and booking conversion standards
  • CRM and booking system usage
  • Client check-in and check-out procedures
  • Payment handling and POS accuracy
  • Upselling and rebooking at reception
  • Complaint handling and escalation rules

Manager & Supervisor Training SOP

Managers are trained to lead, control, and optimize performance.

  • Service and sales standard enforcement
  • Staff supervision and coaching techniques
  • KPI tracking and performance reviews
  • Daily operational control checklists
  • Handling escalations and service recovery
  • Motivating teams and managing discipline

Ensures professional communication, accuracy, and revenue control.

4 Performance, KPIs & Accountability SOP

Clear expectations and measurable performance standards for all roles.

  • Individual and team KPI definitions
  • Sales, rebooking, and service quality targets
  • Attendance, punctuality, and behavior standards
  • Performance evaluation forms
  • Incentive and corrective action frameworks

Ensures staff understand what success looks like — and how it is measured.

Training Methodology

Our training system follows a structured, results-driven approach:

  • Orientation & Standards Explanation

    Clear introduction to expectations and service standards

  • Demonstration & Role-Play Scenarios

    Practical exercises with real-world situations

  • Supervised Practice

    Guided application with feedback and coaching

  • Performance Evaluation & Scoring

    Objective assessment of skills and competencies

  • SOP Acknowledgment & Sign-Off

    Formal documentation and accountability

Training can be delivered for: New staff onboarding, Existing team retraining, Premium upgrade or re-launch, Ongoing performance improvement
