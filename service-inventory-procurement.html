What Is Included in the Inventory & Procurement System?

Our framework is built on 5 essential control pillars required for profitable and scalable salon operations:

1. Inventory Management SOPs

Clear procedures that control how products are received, stored, used, and monitored.

  • Inventory receiving and verification SOP
  • Storage, labeling, and FIFO control SOP
  • Daily product usage and issuing SOP
  • Retail product control and POS alignment SOP
  • Wastage, damage, and expiry control SOP
  • Inventory counting and reconciliation SOP

2. Inventory Control Checklists & Reports

Daily, weekly, and monthly tools that keep inventory under control at all times.

  • Daily stock level checklist
  • Service product availability checklist
  • Retail display and reconciliation checklist
  • Weekly inventory usage report
  • Monthly inventory value and wastage report

3. Product Ordering & Reorder System

A structured ordering process that prevents overstocking, shortages, and cash flow pressure.

  • Minimum and maximum stock level setup
  • Reorder point calculations based on usage
  • Monthly ordering planning checklist
  • Purchase order (PO) creation and approval SOP
  • Emergency ordering control SOP

4. Full Procurement Management SOP

End-to-end procurement governance covering suppliers, purchasing, receiving, and payments.

  • Procurement planning and budget control SOP
  • Supplier selection and approval SOP
  • Purchase requisition (PR) and authorization process
  • Purchase order (PO) management SOP
  • Goods receiving and quality inspection SOP
  • Invoice matching and payment control SOP
  • Capital equipment and high-value procurement SOP

5. Supplier & Cost Control Documentation

Tools to manage supplier performance and protect margins.

  • Approved supplier register
  • Supplier price comparison sheets
  • Procurement spend and variance reports
  • Supplier performance evaluation scorecards
