What Is Included in the Salon Documentation System?

Our documentation framework is built on 5 essential operational pillars required for high-performing salons:

1. Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Clear, practical procedures that ensure consistency, quality, and compliance.

  • Salon opening and closing SOPs
  • Client journey & consultation SOPs
  • Service delivery standards
  • Hygiene, sanitation, and safety SOPs
  • Complaint handling & service recovery SOPs
  • Cash handling, POS, and financial control SOPs
  • Management and supervision SOPs

2. Operational Checklists

Daily, weekly, and monthly checklists that ensure standards are followed — not forgotten.

  • Daily opening & closing checklists
  • Service room & workstation checklists
  • Hygiene & sanitation checklists
  • Staff attendance & readiness checklists
  • Inventory & stock control checklists
  • Manager daily control checklist

3. Management & Performance Reports

Professional reporting tools to track performance, profitability, and efficiency.

  • Daily sales and revenue reports
  • Staff performance & KPI reports
  • Client feedback & satisfaction reports
  • Inventory usage and wastage reports
  • Incident & complaint logs
  • Monthly profit and performance summaries

4. Staff Control & Accountability Documents

Documentation that supports structure, discipline, and performance culture.

  • Staff role descriptions
  • KPI tracking sheets
  • Service quality evaluation forms
  • Staff onboarding and training records
  • Manager sign-off and audit forms

5. Launch, Upgrade & Audit Documentation

Tools to support smooth openings, re-launches, and ongoing control.

  • Pre-opening readiness checklist
  • Re-launch & upgrade execution checklist
  • First 30–60–90 day control reports
  • Internal audit and compliance checklists
